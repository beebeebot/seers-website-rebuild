AI Visibility

How LLMs decide who to recommend

  • Entity clarity — Organisation schema, clear "About" content, named team members. LLMs need to know who you are.
  • Citeable content — Structured, factual statements with specifics. Not marketing fluff — real expertise AI can quote.
  • Topical authority — Regular, deep content on your core topics signals you're a genuine authority in the space.
  • Technical accessibility — An llms.txt file, clean HTML, and proper crawler access let AI systems actually read your site.

Our current AI readiness score: 10 out of 100. A rebuild takes us to best-in-class.
