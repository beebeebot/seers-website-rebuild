We deliver $50M+ in technology services to Government and Enterprise. Our website should reflect that calibre — and right now, it doesn't.

SEO + GEO

One investment, two channels

Stephen's right — people are increasingly using AI to find services. But here's the thing: the work that improves your Google ranking is the same work that gets you mentioned by ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

Structured data, clear entity definitions, citeable content, and an llms.txt file all help AI models understand who we are and what we do. When someone asks an LLM "Who does government IT consulting in Australia?" — we want to be in the answer.

6

AI Visibility

How LLMs decide who to recommend

  • Entity clarity — Organisation schema, clear "About" content, named team members. LLMs need to know who you are.
  • Citeable content — Structured, factual statements with specifics. Not marketing fluff — real expertise that AI can quote.
  • Topical authority — Regular, deep content on your core topics signals you're an authority in the space.
  • Technical accessibility — An llms.txt file, clean HTML, and proper crawler access let AI systems actually read your site.

Our current AI readiness score: 10 out of 100. A rebuild takes us to best-in-class.
